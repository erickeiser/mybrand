Pig-Dice Game

View Project View Code

Here we have the classic Pig dice game where you have to roll the dice to get your current round score as high as possible. When you decide to hold your current score will be added to your global score. First player to 100 points wins. Be careful not to roll a 1 or you will lose all of your current points and then next player will have an opportunity to to roll the dice.
